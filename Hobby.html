Hobbies and Interest

Shooting guns

I didn’t grow up around guns, so this hobby of mine is new.
Once I joined the Marines though, I soon realized how fun it is to just
go to the range and shoot for a couple of hours. It is a rather expensive
hobby, so I don’t go too often as I am a rather frugal person, but when I do go, I have an absolute blast!

Investing

Investing and researching the market is probably where I spend most of my
time. I find it so interesting watching asset classes shift based on real-time factors playing out in the world.
I probably enjoy it the most of any of my hobbies as it has the largest
impact on my life and the possibility to dramatically change my financial situation. I do play it relatively safe though since the riskier my investments are the less sleep I tend to get.

Working out with my wife

Working out with my wife is not only something I look forward to every day
but also something that I find therapeutic. It helps me and my wife
bond over something we both enjoy. Whether it’s a hard workout or even if it’s
just yoga on a rest day, I find that it gives me and my wife the stress relief we both need with our rigorous lives.

Video Games

With my life being so busy now, it is very hard to find time to play video games.
Though, if I were able to, I would play video games with my friends for hours a day.
I absolutely love video games and they are something I will never outgrow. Eventually,
there will come a time in my life when I can play them when I want to but for now, I have obligations and responsibilities I must take care of.
